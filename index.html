Discounting

Define \( f \) as the forward/spot denominated in FOR-DOM (e.g. USD/JPY). Then \[ f = e^{(r_{d}-r_{f})t} \] In the case where cross-currency basis swap spread is quoted as xIBOR spreads. Then \[ f = e^{(L_{u}-(L_{j}+B))t} \] For discounting USD flows in under JPY CSA we hence use \[ e^{c_{f}t} = \frac{e^{O_{j}t}}{f} = e^{(O_{j}-L_{u}+(L_{j}+B))t}\] For discounting JPY flows in under USD CSA we hence use \[ e^{c_{d}t} = f{e^{O_{u}t}} = e^{(O_{u} + L_{u}-(L_{j}+B))t} \] In a nutshell, a discounting rate \( r \)for currency X in collateral Y would be \[ r_{x} + (r'_{x} - r'_{y})\] where \( r' \) is the cross currency funding rate
